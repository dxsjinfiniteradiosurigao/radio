About DXSJ

DXSJ 93.3 Infinite Radio Surigao is a leading FM radio station serving Surigao City and the neighboring communities of Surigao del Norte. Dedicated to delivering quality entertainment, timely news, public affairs, and community-centered programming, the station has become a trusted source of information and inspiration for thousands of listeners across the region.

As part of the growing Infinite Radio Network, DXSJ 93.3 FM combines modern broadcasting technology with a strong commitment to public service. The station features a diverse lineup of programs that include local news, current affairs, music, public discussions, educational content, and special community events designed to keep listeners informed, engaged, and connected.

Driven by its mission to provide responsible broadcasting and meaningful content, DXSJ continues to uphold the values of integrity, professionalism, and community involvement. Through radio, digital platforms, and social media, Infinite Radio Surigao remains committed to being the voice of the community and a reliable companion for every Surigaonon.
